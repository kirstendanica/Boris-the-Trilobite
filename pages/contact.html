Creator

My name is Kirsten, and I'm the mind behind Boris & his little website.

Some random information about me:

  • I've always loved roly-poly bugs (pill bugs) and thought they were so cool / cute. I suspect this is what attracted me to trilobites right away!
  • When I was little, I wanted to create a video game so badly and learn how to do it, but I was discouraged by being told that it "involved a lot of math and hard stuff" - now that I've gotten so into web development and learning more and more, I have no fear of one day making that game one day. ☺
  • My career started off as an English teacher because my best subject in school was always English / Language Arts, and I really wanted to travel to teach English in Saint-Petersburg, Russia (I did!)
  • When I'd first learned about paleontology and natural history as a child, I thought it would be a cool idea to draw pictures of dinosaurs / ancient marine reptiles / etc. & sent these drawings around my neighborhood .. why? I have no idea, maybe I wanted people to be 'reminded' of what was here before? ☺
  • My favorite ancient animal (besides the trilobite) is plesiosaur, and I actually have a tattoo of a plesiosaur!
  • Relating to the point above regarding creating a video game, I've always been super artistic and I have been drawing since I could pick up a writing utensil. I stopped for a long time while pursuing and a bit during my teaching career, because I was discouraged from creating after feeling like I had no 'purpose' for my art.
  • I started drawing trilobites a lot when I left Russia in 2022 because of the war. I had to abandon most of my fossils with friends for the time being, and I wanted to maintain my connection to the community that I was apart of without having any new trilobite fossils to gush over. With nothing but a backpack, art stuff and 2 of my favorite trilobite fossils, I just started to draw them!
