I came up with the idea to create "Boris the Trilobite" for several reasons.
  1. A love for animals, all animals, and paleontology. My all-time favorite is, of course, the trilobite!
  2. While studying web development, I knew that my ultimate personal project would be related to my hobby.
  3. Transitioning careers from a teacher to a developer, I couldn't help but want to share information and educate others on this slightly obscure topic that I love so much.

Why trilobites? An entire website dedicated to them?
